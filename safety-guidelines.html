General Safety Tips for All Rentals
Verify Before You Rent

Always verify the item's condition, documents, and authenticity before accepting it. Take photos of any existing damage.

Use Secure Payments

Never pay cash outside the platform. Always use MyRental's secure payment system to protect your transactions.

Communicate Through Platform

Keep all communication within the MyRental messaging system. Avoid sharing personal contact details early.

Report Issues Immediately

If you encounter any safety issues, report them immediately through the dispute system or emergency contacts.
