My Rental Marketplace ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform.

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

2.2 Listing Information

  • Item/service descriptions, images, and pricing
  • Location and availability information

2.3 Automatically Collected Information
