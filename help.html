Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

To create an account, click on the "Sign up" button in the header. You can sign up using your email address or phone number. After registration, verify your email to activate your account.

How do I book a rental item?

Browse listings on the homepage or search for specific items. Click on a listing to view details, select your dates, and click "Reserve now". Complete the payment to confirm your booking.

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, you can cancel your booking. Cancellation policies vary by listing. Free cancellation is available up to 24 hours before check-in for most listings. Check the listing details for specific cancellation terms.

How do I become a host?

Click on "Become a Host" in the navigation menu. Complete your host profile, verify your identity, and start creating listings. You can list items for rent and start earning money.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept major credit cards, debit cards, and digital payment methods. All payments are processed securely through our payment gateway.

How do I contact the owner?

You can contact the owner through the messaging system. Click "Contact Host" on any listing page to start a conversation. All messages are kept within the platform for safety.

What should I do in case of an emergency?

In case of an emergency, go to "Emergency Contacts" in your account menu. You can report emergencies, contact support, or reach out to local authorities if needed. For immediate assistance, call emergency services.
